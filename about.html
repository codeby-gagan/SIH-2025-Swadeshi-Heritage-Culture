Our Vision & Story

Dharohar was born from a passion for preserving and promoting India's rich cultural heritage. Our vision is to create a dynamic digital platform that connects people, especially the youth, with the stories, traditions, and art forms that define India. We aim to make heritage accessible, engaging, and relevant for generations to come.

Founded in 2023, Dharohar started as a small project to digitize local museum archives. It quickly grew into a comprehensive platform, collaborating with artists, historians, and cultural institutions across the country. Our journey is one of continuous exploration and a deep-rooted commitment to celebrating the soul of India.
